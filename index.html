Legal Services Pricing

A breakdown of legal services and their associated costs in Dubai by Atif Ali Khan depending on the complexity of the legal issue.

Legal Consultation

Initial consultations with advocates in Dubai by Atif Ali Khan can range from AED 500 to AED 1,500, depending on the lawyer’s experience and the complexity of the legal issue.

Document Drafting

  • Contracts and Agreements: AED 2,000 to AED 10,000
  • Wills and Trusts: AED 3,000 to AED 15,000

Litigation

  • Civil Litigation: AED 20,000 retainer, hourly rates from AED 1,000 to AED 3,000
  • Criminal Defense: From AED 50,000 depending on case complexity

Corporate Services

  • Company Formation: AED 10,000 to AED 50,000
  • Corporate Governance: Retainers ranging from AED 5,000 to AED 20,000 per month

Family Law

  • Divorce Proceedings: AED 10,000 to AED 50,000
  • Child Custody and Support: AED 20,000 to AED 60,000

Child Custody and Support

Legal fees for child custody and support cases can vary, with initial consultations costing AED 1,000 to AED 2,000 and overall fees ranging from AED 20,000 to AED 60,000.
